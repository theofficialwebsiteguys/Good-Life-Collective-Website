Good Life Collective

Family-owned. Community-minded. Professionally curated.

While Good Life Collective is new to the City of Rochester, it is not new to the cannabis industry. The Hernandez Family, who owns and operates Good Life Collective, also owns and operates a Good Life Collective retail store in Astoria, Oregon.

Hiram Hernandez, Jr. and his three adult children will each play a role in managing the retail enterprises with the goal of providing the opportunity for the Hernandez family and others involved in this venture to experience—just that—the Good Life.

We are excited to extend that Good Life to our staff, customers, and the community at large.
